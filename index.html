Why This Exists

The interesting work is no longer only model capability.

It is perception, coordination, judgment, instrumentation, trust, and operational reality. Can an agent understand the machine it inhabits? Can multiple agents share reality instead of talking past one another? Can a workflow remain legible as it becomes more autonomous?

Local Frontier exists to document that territory with taste and honesty: not as hype, not as content sludge, but as field notes from people actually living with these systems.
